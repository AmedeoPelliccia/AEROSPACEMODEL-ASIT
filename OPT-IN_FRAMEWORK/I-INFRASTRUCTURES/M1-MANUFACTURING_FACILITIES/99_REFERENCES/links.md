## Quality Standards

### ISO 9001 — Quality Management Systems
- **Organization:** International Organization for Standardization (ISO)
- **URL:** https://www.iso.org/standard/62085.html
- **Description:** Requirements for quality management systems
- **Latest Edition:** ISO 9001:2015
- **Relevance to M1:** Foundation for aerospace QMS

### AS9100 — Aerospace Quality Management
- **Organization:** SAE International
- **URL:** https://www.sae.org/standards/content/as9100d/
- **Description:** Quality management systems for aerospace industry
- **Latest Edition:** AS9100 Rev D (2016)
- **Relevance to M1:** Mandatory for aerospace production organizations

### AS9120 — Aerospace and Defense Distributors Quality Management
- **Organization:** SAE International
- **URL:** https://www.sae.org/standards/content/as9120b/
- **Description:** Quality management for aerospace distributors and suppliers
- **Latest Edition:** AS9120 Rev B (2016)
- **Relevance to M1:** Supply chain qualification

### AS9102 — First Article Inspection
- **Organization:** SAE International
- **URL:** https://www.sae.org/standards/content/as9102b/
- **Description:** Requirements for first article inspection (FAI)
- **Latest Edition:** AS9102B (2016)
- **Relevance to M1:** New part qualification

### NADCAP — Special Process Accreditation
- **Organization:** Performance Review Institute (PRI)
- **URL:** https://www.p-r-i.org/nadcap
- **Description:** Accreditation for aerospace special processes
- **Processes:** Welding, NDT, Heat Treatment, Surface Treatment, Composites
- **Relevance to M1:** Critical process qualification

### ISO 10007 — Configuration Management
- **Organization:** International Organization for Standardization (ISO)
- **URL:** https://www.iso.org/standard/70400.html
- **Description:** Guidelines for configuration management
- **Latest Edition:** ISO 10007:2017
- **Relevance to M1:** Engineering change control
