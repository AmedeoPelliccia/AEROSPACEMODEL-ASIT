## Equipment Inspection and Maintenance

| Equipment | Inspection Interval | Responsible |
|---|---|---|
| Wheel chocks | Weekly visual; before each use | Maintenance |
| Tie-down straps / chains | Annual load test; before each use visual | Maintenance |
| GPU | Per manufacturer's maintenance schedule | Ground Ops |
| H₂ fixed detectors | Weekly bump test; 6-month calibration | Safety Officer |
| H₂ portable detectors | Pre-use bump test; 6-month calibration | Safety Officer |
| Boil-off recovery connection | Annual leak test | Safety Officer |
