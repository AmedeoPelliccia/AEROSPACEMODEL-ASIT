## Earning Criteria

Tokens are awarded upon verified completion of an eligible activity
(KNOT closure, requirement authoring, test execution, or safety analysis).
All awards require STK_CM confirmation.

## Vesting Schedule

Awarded tokens vest immediately upon recording in `TT_DISTRIBUTION.csv`.
No lock-up period applies.

## Redemption

Token redemption follows programme-level rules defined in the
OPT-IN Framework governance. Tokens are non-transferable between
ATA domains without CCB approval.
