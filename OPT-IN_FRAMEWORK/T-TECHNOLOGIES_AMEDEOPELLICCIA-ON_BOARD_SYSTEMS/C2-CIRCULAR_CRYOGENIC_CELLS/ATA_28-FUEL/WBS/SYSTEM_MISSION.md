# ATA 28 – System Mission

**System:** AMPEL360 Q100 LH₂ Fuel System (C2 Circular Cryogenic Cells)
**ATA Chapter:** 28 – Fuel
**Version:** 1.0.0

## Mission Statement

Provide safe, reliable cryogenic hydrogen fuel storage and delivery for the
AMPEL360 Q100 hydrogen-electric aircraft, enabling zero-emission aviation
through novel C2 circular cryogenic cell technology.

## Key Drivers

- **Safety:** Ensure hydrogen containment integrity across all flight phases.
- **Performance:** Maintain cryogenic temperatures with minimal boil-off loss.
- **Reliability:** Achieve dispatch reliability targets for commercial operations.
- **Sustainability:** Enable carbon-free propulsion through hydrogen fuel.
- **Certification:** Meet EASA CS-25 with Special Conditions SC-28-H2-001 and SC-28-CRYO-002.
