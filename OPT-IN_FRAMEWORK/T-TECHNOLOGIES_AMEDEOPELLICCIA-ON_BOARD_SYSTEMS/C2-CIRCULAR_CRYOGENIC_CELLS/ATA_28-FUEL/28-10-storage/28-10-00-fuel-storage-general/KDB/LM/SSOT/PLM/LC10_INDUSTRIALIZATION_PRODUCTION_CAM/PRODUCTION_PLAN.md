# Production Plan — 28-10-00

## Manufacturing Process

| Step | Process | Key Parameter |
|------|---------|--------------|
| 1 | Inner shell forming | 316L deep draw / spin forming |
| 2 | Welding | TIG welding, inert atmosphere |
| 3 | MLI wrapping | 40-layer automated wrapping |
| 4 | Outer shell assembly | 5083 Al alloy fabrication |
| 5 | Vacuum jacket creation | Pump-down and seal |
| 6 | Leak test | He leak test < 1E-9 mbar·L/s |

## Supply Chain

Critical materials: 316L sheet, 5083 Al alloy, MLI blankets.
All suppliers require cryogenic material certification.

## Production Rate Target
