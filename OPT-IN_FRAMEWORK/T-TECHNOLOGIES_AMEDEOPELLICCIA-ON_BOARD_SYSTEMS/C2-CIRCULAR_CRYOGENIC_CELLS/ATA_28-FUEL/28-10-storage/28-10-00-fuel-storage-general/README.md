# 28-10-00 Fuel Storage General

**Subject:** General fuel storage design, specifications, and safety for
AMPEL360 Q100 C2 Circular Cryogenic Cells.

**ATA:** 28-10-00 — Fuel Storage  
**Technology Domain:** C2 – Circular Cryogenic Cells  
**Contract:** KITDM-CTR-LM-CSDB_ATA28  
**Lifecycle Phase:** LC04 (Design Definition)  
**Baseline:** DBL-Q100-2026-Q2

## Scope

This subject covers the general design, material selection, insulation
strategy, and safety framework for the C2 circular cryogenic hydrogen
storage cells used in the AMPEL360 Q100 fuel system.
